ATS INFRA stands at the forefront of defense infrastructure innovation, delivering cutting-edge solutions that redefine resilience in hostile environments. Our proprietary technologies and strategic engineering approaches ensure operational continuity under the most extreme conditions.

With a legacy of excellence spanning decades, we've cultivated a global reputation for delivering infrastructure that exceeds the highest military standards. We don't just build structures—we engineer confidence in the face of adversity, ensuring that our clients maintain strategic advantage through superior infrastructure resilience.

Steel Sandwich

Advanced composite structures with exceptional strength-to-weight ratios, designed for rapid deployment in hostile environments with maximum protection. Our proprietary layering technology creates impenetrable barriers that withstand extreme conditions while maintaining structural integrity.

The innovative sandwich design incorporates multiple protective layers that work in synergy to dissipate impact energy and prevent penetration, making it ideal for forward operating bases and critical infrastructure protection.

Kevlar Textile Concrete

Innovative material combining ballistic fiber technology with concrete matrices for blast-resistant structures with enhanced durability and impact absorption. This revolutionary composite withstands multiple ballistic impacts while maintaining structural stability.

Our Kevlar-reinforced concrete provides superior protection against fragmentation and blast waves, making it the ideal choice for perimeter security, bunkers, and protective shelters in high-risk environments.

Lattice Structures

Complex geometric structures optimized for maximum strength with minimal material usage, providing superior blast and ballistic protection in lightweight configurations. Our algorithmic design ensures optimal energy dissipation across the entire structure.

The lattice framework allows for rapid deployment and modular construction while providing exceptional resistance to explosive forces and projectile impacts, making it perfect for temporary military installations and rapid response scenarios.

Ballistic Resistance

Advanced protective solutions engineered to withstand ballistic threats while maintaining structural integrity and operational functionality.

ORLs - Operational Readiness Levels

Ensuring defense infrastructure meets stringent ballistic protection standards with certified performance metrics. Our ORL certification process exceeds military requirements for operational readiness and structural integrity across all threat levels.

Each ORL classification represents a comprehensive evaluation of material performance, structural resilience, and operational capability under simulated combat conditions, providing commanders with verified protection data for strategic planning.

OJLs - Operational Junction Links

Providing seamless integration between ballistic protection systems and existing infrastructure with enhanced connectivity. Our OJL technology ensures interoperability across defense systems while maintaining maximum protection levels during joint operations.

The OJL framework enables rapid deployment of modular protection systems that can be integrated with existing military infrastructure, reducing setup time while maintaining the highest standards of ballistic and blast protection.

Geophysical Works

Subterranean infrastructure solutions utilizing advanced geophysical technologies for secure and resilient underground facilities.

Underground OPs Room

Secure operational command centers built underground with advanced communication systems, environmental controls, and blast-resistant construction. Our OPs rooms feature electromagnetic pulse protection and redundant systems for continuous operation under extreme conditions.

These facilities are designed to withstand direct attacks while maintaining full operational capability, featuring advanced air filtration, independent power systems, and secure communications infrastructure for command and control operations.

Underground Bomb Shelter

Multi-level protective facilities with integrated life support systems, emergency power, and advanced air filtration for extended occupancy during threats. Our shelters can sustain operations for months without external support in complete isolation.

These shelters incorporate blast-resistant construction, radiation shielding, and comprehensive emergency systems to protect personnel from various threats while maintaining comfortable living conditions for extended durations.

Underground Tunneling Work

Specialized excavation and construction techniques for creating secure underground passageways with structural integrity monitoring and reinforcement. Our tunneling methods minimize surface disruption while maximizing security and structural stability.

Using advanced geological assessment and precision excavation technologies, we create underground networks that provide secure transportation and utility corridors while maintaining the highest standards of structural integrity and safety.

Geophysical Services & Equipment

ERT SURVEY

Electrical Resistivity Tomography for detailed subsurface imaging and geological characterization essential for defense infrastructure planning and security assessment. Our advanced ERT systems provide high-resolution data for underground facility planning.

SRT SURVEY

Seismic Refraction Tomography providing critical data on subsurface rock properties and structural integrity for secure underground facility construction. Our SRT technology delivers precise geological mapping for defense infrastructure projects.

Drone-MAGNETOMETER SURVEY

Advanced aerial magnetic surveys using drone technology for rapid, high-resolution magnetic field mapping essential for defense site assessment and security planning. Our drone-based systems provide comprehensive magnetic anomaly detection.

Drone-AEM SURVEY

Airborne Electromagnetic mapping using advanced drone systems for detailed subsurface conductivity analysis and underground structure detection. Our AEM technology identifies potential security risks and optimal facility locations.

Drone-GRAVITY SURVEY

Precision gravity field measurements using specialized drone equipment for detecting subsurface density variations and identifying potential security risks. Our gravity survey technology provides critical data for underground facility planning.
